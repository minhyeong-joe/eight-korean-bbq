FAQs

If you have any other specific questions, please contact the store directly or click HERE to contact the main office.

DO YOU OPEN FOR HOLIDAYS?

Yes, we are open for all holidays and operate regular hours all year round.

But please be advised that any weekday specials (lunch menu) won't be available on holidays.

All holidays will be considered as weekend days.

HOW MUCH IS IT?

Eight Korean BBQ is NOT All You Can Eat.

We do not charge by person, but by order, and prices vary among different options.

Here's some estimate you can refer to:

  • Combo menus for approx. 3-4 people are around $55 - $75
  • Lunch menu for 2 people is usually $20 - $35

* prices may vary by location

WHERE DO I PARK?

* * * For Buena Park Eight Korean BBQ * * *

We understand that we have limited parking space.

In case, our restaurant parking lot is full, you may use the church parking lot.

We are sorry for the inconvenience, and Thank you for your understanding.
